Where it Started

At the beginning of my studies with Pursuit, one of the administrators suggested to us that we take notes. She said "I promise you you will learn much more if you write down what you've learned after each class as you progress through each week of the program. I took on doing that through out the year.

As we approached the end of the year, I decide to make a web site out of the notes I've taken. This is the result of that effort.

I am a Fellow at Pursuit, an intensive 12-month software engineering Fellowship with a 9% acceptance rate, based in Queens New York (Dec. 2022 to Dec 2023).

My journey into Computer Science and Software Engineering began when an associate who was Computer Programmer told me I "would be good at programming because I have an analytical approach to problem solving". I soon started taking math and computer courses at night after a full day-shift of work.

My skills include software development using vanilla HTML, CSS and Javascript, as well as Desktop Hardware and Software User Support.

Given my passion for music, and cultural development, I would love to be able to bring my web engineering skills to making a real difference for people.

Recently completed web development projects include:

  • GitHub Profile Page
  • Weather App
  • Blog
  • Landing Page
